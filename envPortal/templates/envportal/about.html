    About EnvPortal

    EnvPortal is a website designed to make it easy for anyone to learn about environmental events and resources in their area.
    This site provides several resources to allow users to explore and experience environmental outreach near them.
    One can use the Find Resources feature to search the area near them for various different types of local/community environmental resources, such as lumber mills and recycling centers.
    There is also the Find Events feature, which allows users to view a list of events being run in their area among a variety of categories. Events are viewed by date, allowing users to easily tell what events they could attend.
    If you don't find an event you like, you can feel free to create your own! Users are encouraged to organize their own events using the site, which allows the events to get noticed and get more attendance than the average backyard protest.
